EasyFresh

Our EasyFresh safe guarantees market-freshness in your home. Whether it's unpackaged fruit, vegetables, or other items, everything is stored optimally here. Thanks to the tight seal, the food increases the humidity in the safe, keeping the food fresh for longer.

NoFrost

When you open your freezer, you want to see frozen food - but certainly not ice and frost. NoFrost protects the freezer compartment from unwanted icing, which consumes a lot of energy and can be expensive. NoFrost means no more tedious and time-consuming defrosting of the freezer, giving you more time for other things – and saving money.

DuoCooling

Thanks to two completely separate cooling circuits, DuoCooling ensures that there is no air exchange between the refrigerator and freezer compartments. Food doesn't dry out, and odors don't transfer. This means less waste, less shopping, and more savings and enjoyment.

Retrofitable SmartDeviceBox

SteelFinish

Do you love cool elegance in your kitchen? Then a SMEG with SteelFinish is your choice. The co-laminate material with vertical brushing is visually very similar to real stainless steel, robust, and easy to clean. The silver-painted side panels perfectly complement the elegant look.

Touch display

Truly touching: The touch display makes operating your SMEG simple and intuitive. All functions are clearly arranged on the display. With a light touch of your finger, you can effortlessly select functions or check the current temperature of your refrigerator.

Recessed Grip

Unique and functional: Thanks to the vertically continuous and ergonomically positioned recessed handle, you can open the appliance with ease. Additionally, the modern and puristic design allows for perfect integration into your handle-free kitchen.

InteriorFit

Complete the puristic design of your kitchen: Our refrigerators fit perfectly into a 60cm-deep niche. Only the door protrudes slightly, ensuring optimal access to the recessed handle and lever handle. This keeps the focus on your kitchen – with your SMEG as the focal point and highlight.

PowerCooling system

Do you want to be sure that your refrigerator's cooling is distributed almost evenly? The PowerCooling system ensures this: The powerful yet quiet fan efficiently distributes the cold air throughout the entire refrigerator compartment.

Base concept

Do you want your refrigerator to be powerful and convenient – ​​but also sustainable? Then a SMEG is perfect for you. Our BluPerformance technology makes it a true all-rounder: It offers extra volume and cooling performance – yet consumes extremely little energy. And listen up: You'll hear almost nothing. Because your SMEG is extremely quiet despite its power.

SuperSilent

Door rack
